membership programs

Basic membership

This membership option offers limited access to gym facilities, designed specifically for those who can work out during quieter times. With access restricted to off-peak hours, typically weekdays before 5 PM, members enjoy a more relaxed, peaceful environment, perfect for focusing on their fitness goals without the crowd.

The cost is set at the lowest price point, making it an affordable choice for individuals on a budget who still want quality fitness experiences. This membership is ideal for beginners who are just starting out and may not need full-time access, as well as for those with flexible schedules, like students, retirees, remote workers, or anyone seeking a more economical way to access the gym.

Members can take advantage of a complimentary orientation session with a personal trainer, helping them become comfortable with the equipment and ensuring they start off on the right track. Additionally, select group classes are available during off-peak times, offering variety and motivation to keep workouts interesting.

While this membership provides essential access to core facilities, upgrading to a higher-tier membership is always an option as fitness needs evolve. This plan combines affordability with flexible, quality gym access, offering a seamless entry point into fitness.

premium membership

Our Premium Membership grants unlimited access to all gym facilities, allowing members the freedom to work out anytime that suits their schedule, with no restrictions on peak hours. From early mornings to late nights, members enjoy the flexibility of using all available resources whenever they want.

This membership level includes complimentary access to all group classes, ranging from high-intensity interval training (HIIT) to yoga, Pilates, and more. Members can enjoy an array of diverse class options led by our skilled instructors, catering to all fitness levels and interests. It’s the perfect way to stay motivated, connect with others, and maintain variety in your workout routine.

Premium members also receive personalized fitness guidance through one-on-one sessions with our expert personal trainers. These sessions are tailored to each member’s specific goals, whether they’re looking to build muscle, improve endurance, or enhance overall wellness. Members can schedule these sessions at their convenience to ensure consistent support and results.

As part of our commitment to supporting a holistic approach to health, premium members are granted exclusive access to our wellness programs, which include nutritional counseling, lifestyle coaching, and workshops on healthy living. These additional services are designed to provide comprehensive support beyond the gym floor, helping members achieve balanced and sustainable wellness.
